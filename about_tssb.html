Personalized approach

TSSB Alpha establishes deep strategic cooperation with Malaysian universities to better implement Sino-Malaysian educational exchanges:

  • Signing cooperation agreements (LOI or MOU) for collaboration
  • Establishing a co-research center
  • Appointing visiting professors (2-3 individuals)
  • Arranging for university lecturers to teach at partner universities or lead specific research projects
  • Jointly publishing international journals between Chinese and Malaysian universities
  • Co-hosting international seminars, forums, and lectures between Chinese and Malaysian universities
  • Study exchange: Specialized in-depth visits and face-to-face meetings with renowned university professors
  • Overseas employment: Arranging overseas work for graduates
  • Study abroad services: Consultation, application and registration, visa procedures, and related services.

Message From CEO

Avagrace Law

Avagrace Law is a distinguished leader in education and entrepreneurship. As President of the Malaysia Women Entrepreneurs Chamber of Commerce, she is dedicated to empowering women of all races and fostering economic growth through innovative initiatives and support programs aligned with Malaysia Madani's principles of good governance, sustainable development, and racial harmony.

Her career began in the education sector as a marketing and counselor for foreign student recruitment at KDU University College. She recruited students from diverse regions and helped those who did not initially qualify for academic programs to succeed through conditional admission.

With over 13 years of experience, Avagrace founded TSSB Alpha Sdn Bhd, specializing in study abroad education, academic education, and vocational training. Her company successfully recruits students for prestigious institutions such as Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia, University of Wollongong Malaysia, Universiti Teknologi Petronas (UTP), and Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI).

In her role at MWECC, Avagrace provides technical short courses and government grants to support single mothers and young women entrepreneurs, helping them start businesses or transform their careers. As a Committee Member of AZXCCCM and the former Vice President of MCWEA, she fosters collaboration and growth within the business community.

Avagrace's contributions to education and entrepreneurship are marked by her commitment to excellence, innovative problem-solving, and dedication to empowering individuals and communities. She continues to inspire and lead with passion and purpose, making a significant impact in her professional endeavors.
